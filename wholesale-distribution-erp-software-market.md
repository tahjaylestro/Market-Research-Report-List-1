<p><p>Wholesale distribution ERP software is available in two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise ERP is installed locally on a company's servers, providing control and customization but requiring substantial upfront investment in hardware and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based ERP is hosted remotely, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs as users pay for access via subscriptions. This model allows for easier updates and collaboration, making it increasingly popular among wholesalers looking for efficient distribution management.</p></p>
