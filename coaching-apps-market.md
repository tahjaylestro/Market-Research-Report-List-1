<p><p>Coaching apps can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based coaching apps are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access them via the internet. This model offers flexibility, scalability, and ease of updates. In contrast, on-premises coaching apps require installation on local servers, providing greater control over data and customization but limiting accessibility. Each type caters to different user needs, with cloud-based solutions appealing to organizations prioritizing convenience and on-premises options favored by those needing enhanced security.</p></p>
