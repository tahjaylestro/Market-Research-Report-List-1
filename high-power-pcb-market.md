<p><p>The high power PCB market comprises three main types: single layer, double layer, and multi-layer PCBs. Single layer PCBs offer a straightforward design with one conductive layer, ideal for simple, low-cost applications. Double layer PCBs feature two layers, allowing for more complex circuits while maintaining compactness. Multi-layer PCBs incorporate three or more layers, enabling advanced designs with high density and performance, suitable for demanding applications in telecommunications, automotive, and industrial sectors, where heat dissipation and reliability are crucial.</p></p>
