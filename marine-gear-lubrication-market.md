<p><p>The marine gear lubrication market is divided into two main types: mineral lubricants and synthetic lubricants. Mineral lubricants are derived from refining crude oil, offering good performance and cost-effectiveness for various marine applications. They are often used in traditional vessels. In contrast, synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered to provide superior performance, enhanced stability, and better protection under extreme conditions. They typically offer improved wear resistance and fuel efficiency, making them ideal for modern, high-performance marine engines and gears.</p></p>
