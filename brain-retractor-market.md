<p><ul><li>Cerebrum Retractor</li><li>Cerebellar Retractor</li><li>Brain Stem Retractor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brain retractor market includes specialized instruments designed for neurosurgical procedures, categorized mainly into three types. The cerebrum retractor is used to hold back brain tissue during surgeries involving the cerebral cortex. The cerebellar retractor focuses on providing access to the cerebellum, often necessary for posterior fossa procedures. The brain stem retractor is tailored for procedures involving the brain stem, facilitating clear visibility and access while minimizing trauma to surrounding structures. Each type is crucial for enhancing surgical precision and outcomes.</p></p>
