<p><p>The GigE Industrial Cameras market is primarily classified into two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality and low noise levels, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision and detail, such as in scientific and medical fields. Conversely, CMOS cameras offer faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and greater integration capabilities, making them suitable for real-time imaging and high-speed applications in industrial automation and machine vision.</p></p>
