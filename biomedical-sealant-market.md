<p><p>The biomedical sealant market includes collagen and fibrin types, each with distinct properties and applications. Collagen sealants are derived from natural proteins that promote tissue adhesion and healing, making them ideal for applications in surgery and wound care. Fibrin sealants, derived from blood components, are used to promote clotting and tissue repair, particularly in surgical procedures. Both types enhance wound closure, reduce bleeding, and support the body’s healing process, thereby improving patient outcomes in various medical settings.</p></p>
