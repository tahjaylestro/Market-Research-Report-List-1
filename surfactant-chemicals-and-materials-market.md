<p><strong>The Surfactant Chemicals and Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic</li><li>Cationic</li><li>Amphoteric</li><li>Nonionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surfactant chemicals and materials are categorized into four main types based on their ionic charge: </p><p>1. **Anionic surfactants** carry a negative charge and are effective in cleaning and foaming, commonly found in laundry detergents.</p><p>2. **Cationic surfactants** have a positive charge, often used for their antimicrobial properties and in fabric softeners.</p><p>3. **Amphoteric surfactants** can carry both positive and negative charges, providing versatility in applications like personal care products. </p><p>4. **Nonionic surfactants** lack a charge, making them excellent for compatibility and stability in various formulations, including mild cleaners and emulsifiers.</p></p>
