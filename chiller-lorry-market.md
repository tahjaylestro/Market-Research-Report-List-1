<p><p>The chiller lorry market is primarily divided into two types: single temperature and multi-temperature types. Single temperature lorries maintain a consistent temperature throughout the cargo area, making them ideal for transporting perishable goods that require uniform cooling. In contrast, multi-temperature lorries feature multiple compartments, allowing the transportation of various products at different temperature settings simultaneously. This versatility accommodates a diverse range of goods, such as frozen items alongside fresh produce, enhancing operational efficiency for logistics businesses.</p></p>
