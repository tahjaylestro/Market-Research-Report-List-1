<p><ul><li>Fluorometric</li><li>Colorimetric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Immunoassay kits utilize various detection methods to measure specific antigens or antibodies in samples. Fluorometric immunoassays rely on fluorescent labels that emit light when excited, allowing for sensitive and rapid quantification. Colorimetric assays, on the other hand, produce a color change in the presence of the target analyte, providing a simple visual readout or measurable absorbance. Both types are widely used in clinical diagnostics, research, and environmental testing, each offering unique advantages in terms of sensitivity, ease of use, and cost.</p></p>
