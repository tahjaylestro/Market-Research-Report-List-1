<p><p>The Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) Treatment System Market is categorized into destructive and non-destructive treatment methods. Destructive treatments involve methods that eliminate VOCs by breaking them down, such as thermal oxidation and catalytic oxidation. These systems convert VOCs into less harmful substances, effectively reducing emissions. Non-destructive treatments focus on capturing and recycling VOCs without destroying them, employing techniques like adsorption and absorption. These methods enable the recovery and reuse of valuable compounds, promoting sustainability in industrial processes.</p></p>
