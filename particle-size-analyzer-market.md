<p><strong>The Particle Size Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Diffraction</li><li>Dynamic Light Scattering</li><li>Imaging Analysis</li><li>Coulter Principle</li><li>Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Particle Size Analyzer market comprises various techniques for measuring particle dimensions. Laser Diffraction utilizes laser light to determine size distributions by analyzing scattering patterns. Dynamic Light Scattering measures the Brownian motion of particles to infer size in colloidal systems. Imaging Analysis captures particle images for size and shape assessment. Coulter Principle counts and sizes particles via electrical resistance changes as they pass through a small orifice. Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis visualizes and tracks nanoparticles in liquids, while "Others" includes emerging and niche technologies for particle sizing.</p></p>
