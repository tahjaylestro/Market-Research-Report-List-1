<p><p>The chest tube drainage system market consists of two main types: single chamber and multiple chamber systems. Single chamber systems are straightforward, featuring one compartment that collects fluid or air from the pleural space, making them easy to use and cost-effective. In contrast, multiple chamber systems provide enhanced management, allowing for separation of fluid collection, air removal, and suction. This versatility enables better monitoring and treatment of various respiratory conditions, catering to diverse clinical requirements and preferences in healthcare settings.</p></p>
