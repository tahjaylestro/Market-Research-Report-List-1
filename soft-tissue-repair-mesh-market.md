<p><p>The Soft Tissue Repair Mesh market is divided into two primary types: synthetic and biologic meshes. Synthetic meshes are made from man-made materials like polypropylene or polyester, offering durability and resistance to infection but may have varying biocompatibility. Biologic meshes, derived from natural tissues such as dermis or pericardium, promote tissue integration and healing, reducing the risk of rejection but may come at a higher cost. Both types serve specific applications in hernia repair and soft tissue strengthening.</p></p>
