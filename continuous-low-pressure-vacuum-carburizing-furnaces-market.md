<p><p>Continuous low pressure vacuum carburizing furnaces are categorized into single chamber, double chamber, and multi-chamber types. Single chamber furnaces operate with a single processing zone for carburizing, quenching, and tempering. Double chamber furnaces offer distinct chambers for carburizing and quenching, enhancing process efficiency and cooling rates. Multi-chamber systems further improve productivity by integrating multiple processes, allowing for concurrent treatments. Each type serves specific production needs, balancing throughput, efficiency, and flexibility in heat treatment processes for various industrial applications.</p></p>
