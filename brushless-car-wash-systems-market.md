<p><ul><li>Touchless In-bay Car Wash Systems</li><li>Touchless Conveyor Tunnel Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brushless car wash systems market includes two main types: touchless in-bay car wash systems and touchless conveyor tunnel systems. Touchless in-bay systems use high-pressure water jets and specialized detergents to clean vehicles while remaining stationary, making them ideal for quick washes. In contrast, touchless conveyor tunnel systems guide vehicles through a series of washing processes in a long tunnel configuration, providing a comprehensive cleaning experience. Both systems emphasize minimizing friction and contact to protect vehicle surfaces.</p></p>
