<p><strong>The Decolorization Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Decolorization resins are specialized adsorbents used to remove color and impurities from liquids. Type I resins are strong anion exchange resins, effective for decolorizing solutions with higher concentrations of organic compounds, offering better performance in acidic conditions. Type II resins, being weak anion exchange resins, are suitable for neutral to mildly acidic environments, providing a gentler approach to color removal while capturing larger molecules. Both types serve diverse industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment.</p></p>
