<p><ul><li>ASM-based System</li><li>Pressure Swing Adsorption</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Fuel Tank Inerting System market includes three main types: ASM-based systems, Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), and other technologies. ASM-based systems utilize air separation membranes to reduce oxygen levels in fuel tanks, enhancing safety. PSA systems employ cyclic adsorption to extract oxygen, further minimizing fire risk. Other technologies may involve different inerting methods or hybrid solutions. Together, these systems aim to prevent fuel tank explosions by limiting the presence of flammable gases.</p></p>
