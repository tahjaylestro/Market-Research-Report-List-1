<p><p>The Pneumatic Emergency Ventilator market encompasses various types, primarily Non-Invasive Ventilation (NIV) and Multi-Mode Ventilation. Multi-Mode Ventilation combines different ventilation modes, adapting to patients' varying needs, and ensuring effective treatment across diverse clinical scenarios. Together, these types enhance patient care in emergency settings, improving outcomes and offering flexible respiratory therapies.</p></p>
