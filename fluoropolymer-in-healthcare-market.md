<p><p>Fluoropolymers are specialized polymers known for their chemical resistance, low friction, and thermal stability, making them vital in the healthcare market. Key types include PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), valued for its non-stick properties; PVDF (Polyvinylidene fluoride), known for its strong mechanical properties; FEP (Fluorinated ethylene propylene), which offers clarity and flexibility; and PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy alkane), prized for its thermal resistance. Other fluoropolymers also serve niche applications, enhancing equipment, coatings, and tubes in healthcare settings, ensuring hygienic and safe operations.</p></p>
