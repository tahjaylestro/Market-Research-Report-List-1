<p><strong>The Wood Chippers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Propelled Wood Chipper</li><li>PTO Wood Chipper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood chipper market is primarily divided into two main types: self-propelled wood chippers and PTO (Power Take-Off) wood chippers. Self-propelled wood chippers are equipped with their own engine and mobility features, allowing for easy maneuverability in various terrains, making them ideal for larger jobs. In contrast, PTO wood chippers require a tractor for operation, relying on the tractor's power to operate effectively. They are often used in farming and forestry applications, offering a cost-effective solution for wood processing.</p></p>
