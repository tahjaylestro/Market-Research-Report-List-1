<p><p>Leukemia screening includes various diagnostic methods to detect the disease early. Bone marrow aspiration and biopsy involve extracting bone marrow to identify abnormal blood cell production. Chest X-ray or chest CT scans help visualize potential leukemia impacts on the lungs and surrounding tissues. Spinal taps, or lumbar punctures, assess if leukemia has spread to the central nervous system by collecting cerebrospinal fluid. Each method plays a crucial role in accurate diagnosis and guiding treatment decisions in leukemia management.</p></p>
