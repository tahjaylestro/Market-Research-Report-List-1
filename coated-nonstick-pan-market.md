<p><p>The coated nonstick pan market is primarily classified into two types: PTFE and ceramic. PTFE type pans feature a synthetic polymer coating that offers excellent nonstick properties, heat resistance, and durability, making them popular for everyday cooking. In contrast, ceramic type pans utilize a natural coating derived from silica, providing a non-toxic alternative that is free from chemicals like PFOA and PTFE. Ceramic pans are known for their nonstick performance and appealing aesthetic, though they may require more careful handling to maintain longevity.</p></p>
