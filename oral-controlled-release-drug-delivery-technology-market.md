<p><p>The Oral Controlled Release Drug Delivery Technology Market encompasses various systems that regulate the release of drugs. Diffusion Controlled Release Systems utilize the passive movement of drug molecules through a polymer matrix. Dissolution Controlled Release Systems rely on the drug dissolving at a controlled rate. Osmotically Controlled Release Systems use osmotic pressure to release drugs. Combined systems, such as Dissolution & Diffusion Controlled Release, leverage both mechanisms. Ion Exchange Resins modify drug release through ionic interactions. Hydro-dynamically Balanced Systems maintain buoyancy for sustained release. Other systems include innovative delivery methods and technologies.</p></p>
