<p><p>The Aircraft Smoke Detection and Fire Extinguishing System market is primarily categorized into two types: Heat Sensing and Smoke Sensing. Heat Sensing systems detect changes in temperature due to fire, providing an early warning by identifying abnormal heat levels. Smoke Sensing systems, on the other hand, identify the presence of smoke particles, enabling quicker detection of potential fires. Both technologies are essential for enhancing aircraft safety, ensuring prompt responses to fire hazards during flight operations or on the ground.</p></p>
