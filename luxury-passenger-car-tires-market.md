<p><strong>The Luxury Passenger Car Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The luxury passenger car tires market primarily features two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires consist of layers of fabric that run diagonally, offering a softer ride but less performance and durability. They are often used in vintage or classic cars. Radial tires, on the other hand, have steel belts and fabric plies that run horizontally, providing better handling, longer life, and improved fuel efficiency. Radial tires are the preferred choice for modern luxury vehicles due to their superior performance capabilities.</p></p>
