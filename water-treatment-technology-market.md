<p><p>The water treatment technology market encompasses various filtration and separation methods to purify water. Reverse osmosis employs a semipermeable membrane to remove contaminants, while ultrafiltration utilizes a membrane with larger pores for suspending solids and microorganisms. Microfiltration targets particulates, and electro-dialysis separates ions using electric currents. Gas separation techniques focus on removing dissolved gases from liquids. Each method serves specific applications, offering tailored solutions for industrial, municipal, and residential water purification needs, ensuring safe and clean water access.</p></p>
