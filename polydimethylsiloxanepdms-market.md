<p><p>Polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS) is a silicone polymer widely used in various industries due to its unique properties like thermal stability, flexibility, and low surface energy. The PDMS market is categorized into high viscosity and low viscosity types. High viscosity PDMS is utilized in applications requiring enhanced mechanical strength and barrier properties, such as adhesives and sealants. Conversely, low viscosity PDMS is preferred for applications needing easier processing and better flow characteristics, including coatings, personal care products, and medical devices.</p></p>
