<p><p>The Commercial Undercounter Glasswasher market features two primary types based on waste management systems: Gravity Waste Type and Pumped Waste Type. </p><p>Gravity Waste Type models rely on the natural flow of wastewater through gravity, making them simple and cost-effective for businesses with suitable drainage. In contrast, Pumped Waste Type models utilize a pump to manage wastewater disposal, making them ideal for locations lacking direct drainage access. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to different operational needs in the hospitality sector.</p></p>
