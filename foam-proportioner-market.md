<p><ul><li>Pressure Foam Proportioner</li><li>Ring Pump Foam Proportioner</li><li>In-Line Foam Proportioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foam proportioner market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Pressure Foam Proportioners** utilize high pressure to mix water and foam concentrate, ensuring a consistent foam output for firefighting. </p><p>2. **Ring Pump Foam Proportioners** use a mechanical pump to provide a reliable method of foam injection, ideal for various applications.</p><p>3. **In-Line Foam Proportioners** are integrated into the water supply line, allowing for direct mixing of foam and water during firefighting operations, enhancing efficiency and effectiveness in emergency situations.</p></p>
