<p><strong>The Zero Liquid Discharge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional ZLD System</li><li>Hybrid ZLD System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) market includes two primary types: Conventional ZLD Systems and Hybrid ZLD Systems. Conventional ZLD Systems utilize a series of processes, including evaporation and crystallization, to eliminate liquid waste entirely, often requiring significant energy and capital investment. In contrast, Hybrid ZLD Systems combine various treatment methods, such as membrane technologies and conventional processes, to improve efficiency and reduce costs. This flexibility allows industries to adapt to specific wastewater characteristics, making them increasingly popular across various sectors.</p></p>
