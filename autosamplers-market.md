<p><p>Autosamplers are crucial instruments in laboratories, facilitating automated sample introduction for analytical processes. The LC (Liquid Chromatography) autosamplers are designed for liquid samples, ensuring precise and reproducible injections for various analytical applications, including pharmaceuticals and environmental testing. In contrast, GC (Gas Chromatography) autosamplers cater to gaseous or volatile samples, providing efficient handling and analysis of compounds like solvents and hydrocarbons. Both types enhance laboratory efficiency, reduce human error, and significantly improve throughput in analytical workflows.</p></p>
