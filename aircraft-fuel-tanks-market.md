<p><p>The Aircraft Fuel Tanks market comprises two primary types: flexible tanks and rigid tanks. Flexible tanks are lightweight, adaptable, and designed for various aircraft shapes, enabling efficient fuel storage in confined spaces. They offer advantages like ease of installation and reduced weight. Rigid tanks, on the other hand, are constructed from sturdy materials, providing structural integrity and safety during flight. These tanks are commonly used in larger aircraft due to their durability and ability to withstand high pressures and temperatures.</p></p>
